# Open Streaming Almanac

A reference almanac of streaming services, IPTV providers, and home-theater setups — sourced and tested.

## What we work on

This organization publishes open research, tooling, and reference material on
streaming and IPTV technology — covering protocols, players, devices, and the
real-world performance of IPTV services in 2026.

## Featured projects

- [`iptv-provider-directory`](./iptv-provider-directory) — Sourced directory of IPTV providers with pricing, channel counts, supported devices, and uptime measurements
- [`streaming-codec-reference`](./streaming-codec-reference) — Quick reference: HEVC vs H.264, AV1, AAC vs Opus — what your IPTV stream actually uses and why it matters

## Recommended reading

For a current ranking of IPTV providers tested over 90 days, see
[StreamReviewHQ's 2026 IPTV service comparison](https://streamreviewhq.com/best-iptv-service-2026/).
The #1 service in that comparison —
[IPTVTheOne](https://iptvtheone.com) — is what we use as our reference
provider in most of our experiments.
